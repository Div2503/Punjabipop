Punjabi Pop



                              
                                                                                

These are the 5 Punjabi singers who emerged the most in the industry .

Let's talk anout them separately .

SHUBH

He just got fame in the starting of 2022 only . He caught the public's eyes with just his debut song i.e. "We Rollin" which is my personal favourite too . He has just dropped 5 singles and literally all five are marvelous . His vocals are literally magical . His most liked single is "NO LOVE" and the latest one is "Baller".

                          

A.P. Dhillon

He just got fame in the starting of 2021 during the pandemic . He caught the public's eyes with just his song "Excuses" . He has dropped multiple hit singles . People are a literal fan of his catchy melody . His most liked single is "Excuses" and the latest one is " Summer High". Some of his tracks that I like are -

                          

Sidhu Moose Wala

I would say that he is the real man who contributed the most in uplifting the Punjabi Pop industry. In the starting of his carrer , he made songs just like other singers but his melody was really riveting which caught the people's eyes .Later , he made songs on Indian issues too like Farmer's Revolt , matter of water between Punjab and Haryana .He actually became a youth icon for many beacuse if you listen to his lyrics careful , they have a really deep meaning . His most liked single is "295" and the latest one is "SYL". Sadly we won't be able to see anymore tracks from his side coz he's no more . He died due to a gangwar . Still he has secured a place in many people's hearts.

                          

Diljit Donsanjh

He has been in the Punjabi Pop industry for a while and is an actor too.There's nothing unique about his lyrics or melody but his tracks are such that one can't resist to listen . Currently he's trying to experiment with his music which is turning out to be liked by the audience .He had multiple hits but in my opinion , his best song was "Lover" and his latest album is "Drive Thru"

                          

Guru Randhawa

It has been just before pandemic that he got recognition . Most of his songs follow a soothing slow pitched tone as well as meaningful lyrics . His song genre is mostly related to love only .His best track is "Suit-Suit" and the latest album is "Man of the Moon".
